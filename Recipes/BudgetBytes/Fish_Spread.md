### Fish Spread:  ###
Price: 2
Difficulty: 1
Servings: 4
Prep: 10

Ingredients:

- 1 can of fish of choice (herring, tuna, salmon...)
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 small onion
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper

Instructions :
1. Blend everything together until smooth enough, serve on bread or a medium of choice
